About

  • log|F(z)| = Σ wk log|z − zk| is the logarithmic potential of the charge distribution.
  • Hue = arg(F(z)) encodes the harmonic conjugate (stream function).
  • Equipotential lines appear as brightness level curves; gradient field lines appear as hue level curves.
  • Zeros (red points) are positive charges; poles (blue points) are negative charges.

Controls

  • Click and drag charge points to move them.
  • Toggle charges on/off with the buttons above.
  • Arrow keys to pan; +/− keys to zoom; Enter to apply input changes.
  • Press Enter or click "apply changes" to update after editing any input field.

Predefined objects

  • Constants: e, pi, i.
  • Operations: z*w, z^w, z+w, z−w, z/w, abs(z), sqrt, exp, log, sin, cos, tan, arcsin, arccos, arctan, re, im, conjugate, lambertw.
