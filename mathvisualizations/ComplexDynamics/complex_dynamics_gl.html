Instructions

  • The left plot is the parameter space and the right is the dynamical plane.
  • The white point in the parameter space plot corresponds to the value of "c" to be used for the dynamical plot, and may be dragged.
  • The white point in the dynamical plane is the starting point for a plot of the first 8 iterates. This can also be moved.
  • The arrow keys shift and the +/- keys zoom the plot window.
  • The text boxes to the right of the plots may be used to edit the iteration function, as well as the escape condition. (there are separate escape condition boxes for the dynamical plane and the parameter space to ameliorate problems of numerical stability in certain examples)
  • The use of GPU acceleration limits accuracy beyond a certain level of zoom.
